Which type of events, we can choose from the website?
You can choose dance schools, social nights and events which includes music festival.

Which type of dance style/s is given on the website?
There are many dance style/s given on the website which includes Tango, Salsa, Kizomba, Zouk, Bachata, Merengue, Samba, Conga, Rumba and Others.

How will I find nearby dance schools, social nights or events?
When you visit the website, you’re asked to choose the location. After choosing the location, search the dance schools, social nights or events and your preferred dance style/s, you’ll get a list of the dance schools, social nights or events according to your chosen location.

I’m not able to find any dance schools, social nights or events in the chosen location. What to do now?
If you’re not able to find any dance schools, social nights or events then it means we’re not currently available in that location. You can send a request and we’ll try our best to provide listings from your chosen location. Go to our Contact Us to send a request or you can mail us to info@wingslearn.com

I want to reserve a seat in a dance school for a particular class. How will I do it?
If you know the name of the dance school in which you want to reserve a seat, you can search the name of the dance school from the search bar, then a dance school list will open from which you can choose the dance school and then the dance school details page will open. Choose the no. of seats and then click on Reserve. Your seat will be reserved.

Note: You must be logged in to reserve a seat.

A dance school, social night or event is showing prices but I’m not able to pay?
Right now, we do not accept payments. To pay for the prices you can contact the particular dance school, social night or event on the contact no. given on their details page.

I want to get notifications about a particular dance. How will I get it?
To get notifications, you must be logged in. Then go to Profile and in Preferences you’ll find the dance list from where choose the dance style/s and click Save. You’ll then start getting notifications.
